FMEA/FMECA

Dynamic Positioning (DP) Failure Modes and Effects Analyses (FMEAs) are essential for ensuring vessel safety and redundancy in offshore operations. At RMC, we conduct DP FMEAs in strict accordance with the DNV Recommended Practice RPD-102, which outlines the methodology and reporting standards to achieve compliance and operational assurance. To learn more, visit the official DNV page for DNV RPD-102.
