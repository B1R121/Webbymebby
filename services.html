Engineering & Assurance Services

DP FMEA / FMECA

Independent DP FMEA and FMECA studies performed in accordance with DNV-RP-D102 and applicable class rules. Services include desktop studies, trials attendance, DP proving trials support, and gap analysis for existing vessels.

DP Trials & Assurance

Planning and execution support for annual DP trials, post-modification trials, and charterer acceptance trials. Focused on verification of redundancy, fault tolerance, and operational readiness.

Risk & Reliability Studies

HAZID, HAZOP, and technical risk assessments for marine and offshore assets. Structured approaches to identify, assess, and mitigate operational and safety risks.

Class & Client Compliance

Support with class submissions, charterer technical queries, and regulatory compliance. Review and development of technical documentation aligned with flag, class, and client requirements.
